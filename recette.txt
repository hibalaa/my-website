### **1. Spaghetti Carbonara (Italy)**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 400g spaghetti  
- 2 large eggs  
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated  
- 150g pancetta (or bacon), diced  
- 2 cloves garlic, minced  
- Salt and black pepper  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  

**Instructions**:  
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.  
2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together.  
3. Heat olive oil in a pan, cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.  
4. Add cooked spaghetti to the pan. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg mixture. Add reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.  
5. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve hot.

### **2. Chicken Tikka Masala (India)**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 500g chicken breast, cubed  
- 1 cup plain yogurt  
- 2 tbsp garam masala  
- 1 tbsp paprika  
- 1 tsp turmeric  
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil  
- 1 onion, diced  
- 3 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated  
- 2 cups tomato puree  
- 1 cup heavy cream  
- Salt and pepper  

**Instructions**:  
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garam masala, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper for at least 2 hours.  
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, cook chicken until browned. Set aside.  
3. In the same pan, heat remaining oil, saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.  
4. Add tomato puree, cook for 5 minutes. Stir in cream and simmer.  
5. Add chicken back to the sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes. Serve with rice or naan.

### **3. Sushi Rolls (Japan)**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 2 cups sushi rice  
- 2 1/2 cups water  
- 3 tbsp rice vinegar  
- 1 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tsp salt  
- Nori sheets  
- Fillings: cucumber, avocado, cooked shrimp, or raw fish  

**Instructions**:  
1. Rinse rice, cook with water. Mix vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold it into the cooked rice.  
2. Place a nori sheet shiny side down on a bamboo mat. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori.  
3. Add fillings like cucumber, avocado, and shrimp in a line.  
4. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat. Slice into pieces with a sharp knife.  
5. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

### **4. Pancakes (USA)**  
**Ingredients**:  
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp sugar  
- 1 1/4 cups milk  
- 1 egg  
- 3 tbsp melted butter  

**Instructions**:  
1. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl.  
2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter. Combine with dry ingredients.  
3. Heat a pan, lightly grease it, and pour batter to form pancakes.  
4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.  
5. Serve with syrup, butter, or fruit.
