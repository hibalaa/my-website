Chicken Tikka Masala (India)

Chicken Tikka Masala is a rich and aromatic curry, blending tender chicken with a creamy spiced sauce for a truly authentic taste of India.

Food is a powerful way to understand and celebrate culture. Recipes like Chicken Tikka Masala tell the story of India's rich history, blending local ingredients with global influences to create a dish that's enjoyed worldwide.

Cook time

10 minutes

Serving size

2 - 4 Persons

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garam masala, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper for at least 2 hours.

2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan, cook chicken until browned. Set aside.

3. In the same pan, heat remaining oil, sautÃ© onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.

4. Add tomato puree, cook for 5 minutes. Stir in cream and simmer.

5. Add chicken back to the sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes. Serve with rice or naan.
